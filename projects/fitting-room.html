Fitting Room

Aliverti Fitting Room grew out of a simple goal: create content people actually want to watch. Rather than lead with product, we built a styling-led video series that feels like advice from a friend – relatable, low-key, and genuinely useful. From "How to Style a Navy T-Shirt" to "Three Looks, One Day," each episode lets the brand connect without ever feeling like an ad.

Concept, styling, editing & videography: Gabriella Aliverti & Talia Novella
