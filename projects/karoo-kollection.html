The Karoo Kollection

The Karoo Kollection is a celebration of handmade Italian leather boots. The boots carried a distinctly Western feel, and we wanted to honour that while keeping the shoot unmistakably South African – which is where the Karoo came in. The result plays with raw, earthy textures: terracotta pots, sand, dried grasses, and warm natural tones that bridge the rugged American West with the South African landscape.

Our direction was shaped as much by trend research as by the products themselves. Tracking what was driving Winter 2026, we saw the cowboy aesthetic emerging as a clear cultural moment – which made it a natural fit for boots already rooted in a Western sensibility. Leaning into it was a deliberate choice: it let us speak to our established audience while opening a door to a younger demographic we wanted to reach.

Concept, styling & editing: Talia Novella & Gabriella Aliverti
