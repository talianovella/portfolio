Summer Handbags

Inspired by the relaxed glamour of an Italian summer, this handbag capsule collection was shot on location at Bakoven – where raffia totes, breton stripes, and perfect beach lighting meet rock, sea and sand. The result blends Mediterranean ease with the unmistakable feel of a Cape Town summer.
